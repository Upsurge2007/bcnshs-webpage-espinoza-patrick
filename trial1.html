About Us

Bais City National Science High School was built in 1996, located at Tavera Street, Bais City. After many years at that location, Bais City National Science High School was moved to Maria Longga Drive, Brgy. Hangyad, Bais City.

Through the years the school has maintained its status in producing students with outstanding knowledge and life skills. Winning multiple awards in competitions related to science, math, art, and even technology.

The school is not only known to be the best in shaping the students academically but they are also known to have the most respectful students. Maintaining good behavior keeps the school’s healthy environment — away from chaos.

To sum it all up, BCNSHS is a place where students learn and discover their capabilities. A place with a healthy environment that helps students focus on their studies.
